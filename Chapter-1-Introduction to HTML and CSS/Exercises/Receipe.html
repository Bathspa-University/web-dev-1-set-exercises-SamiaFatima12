MACRONI RECEIPE

Yummiest yet simplest

SUMMARY

This is my favourite receipe so far i can just eat it all day and nightbut it must be spicy enough to just make me cry.Not a healthy food to eat constantly in your diet but still who cares favourite is favourite I can just have it all time wihtout days off.The taste the texture of macroni is just yummm I mean its so creamy texture and easy to make.I can assure you that every age group can have it and just enkoy it. Let it be creamy macroni,spicy macroni,white sause macroni,pink sause macroni. Like how much variation we have in only one dish.

Method:

First,take a sausepan and boil the water put macroni with one big table spoon of oil and stir it and put it on medium flame let it cook.After 30 minutes seperate the water from it and let macroni be cool till than take a pan for the sause. Add 3 big spoon of oil let oil be enough hot to cook the ingredients put one onion four tomato and saute it until tomatos make a paste than add garlic gibger paste with one and half spoon of salt add red sause as per my taste Im adding 2 medium spoon of it and some of soya sause after that put macroni in it stir it than add red chlli half spoon,half spoon of garam maslla,tea spoon of dried red chlli,with some pinch of papricka and black pepper mix them very well that all spices get mix into it. Coming to conclusion add some carrot and capsicum for crunch again mix them than layer them with some shredded cheese and top of it sprinkle some oregeno and coriander leaves for some tasty smell and for good look and texture.
